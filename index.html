About Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, mobile devices, networks, and data from hackers, malware, phishing, and other cyber attacks. It helps keep personal and business information safe.

Common Cyber Threats

🦠 Malware

Malware can damage computers, steal information, and slow down systems.

🎣 Phishing

Phishing scams trick people into sharing passwords and personal details through fake emails or websites.

🔒 Network Security

Firewalls, antivirus software, and secure passwords help protect computer networks.
